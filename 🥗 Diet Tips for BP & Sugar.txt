🥗 Diet Tips for BP & Sugar

❌ Avoid sugar, white bread, and white rice

✅ Eat more fiber, green vegetables, and fruits

🧂 Reduce salt, fried food, and junk snacks

💧 Drink at least 2 liters of water daily

🚶 Walk 30 minutes daily or do light exercise

🚭 No smoking or alcohol

🧘‍♀️ Avoid stress – try meditation, music, or nature walks

⏰ Follow a consistent sleep & meal routine
